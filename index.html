Abinash Rout

Abinash Rout, a native of Kendujhar, Odisha, is a multifaceted Indian film director and producer who has made a name for himself in the bustling cities of Mumbai and Hyderabad. With a prolific career spanning various domains, he has established himself as a versatile and accomplished filmmaker. His body of work includes directing numerous web series, music videos, and TV commercials (100+ TVC ad shoots), earning him several awards such as Best Entrepreneur, Best Director, and Best Debut Assistant Director Awards.

As a seasoned professional, Abinash has also assisted in the direction of various serials and shows, produced content through his own production house, Zoomster Studio, and co-invested in serials. He leads as CEO of Zoomster Hub Private Limited and spearheads the OTT-TV platform, Zoomaax TV. With expertise in working with prominent brands, including jewelry brands, on print shoots and commercials, Abinash showcases his versatility in diverse formats. His dedication to excellence and innovative storytelling has solidified his position as a leading filmmaker in the Indian entertainment industry, making him a notable figure in Mumbai and Hyderabad's film circuits.
